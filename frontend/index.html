The Collective Unconscious is an online installation with the sole goal of tracking and showing how much time is wasted.

What is the collective unconscious?

The collective unconscious, in this setting, refers to the amount of time spent not-thinking-- not-contributing, not-improving. Time spent online, unconscious.

What am I supposed to do?

Whenever you're not being productive (which, don't lie, is probably pretty often), just open up thecollectiveunconscious.org. The time you spend on the page will be added to the total tally, which is the big number you'll see on the middle of the page when you exit and close this window.

You're misusing Jung's term!

We know. This installation is about a different type of collective unconscious.

Will it lag me?

It's very unlikely that thecollectiveunconscious.org will cause any performance issues. Every five seconds it sends and receives a very small packet of information from the Collective Unconsciousnes servers. This packet is so small that it's almost impossible that you'd notice it.
So go on, waste some more time.
